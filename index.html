Problem

In order for Yelp to give users reliable and trustworthy reviews and ratings, Yelp has created a category of users known as Elite users. Now you might be asking yourself, what does it take to become an elite Yelper. Well there are three basic qualites that Yelp is looking for in Yelpers in order to tag them as Elite, and they are Authenticity, Contribution and Connection. Now the main problem for Yelp is that according to their website, every year they scour every Yelp profile to determine whether a Yelper is Elite or not. We found this to be inefficent and time consuming, so we decided to make it eaiser for Yelp and narrow down the candidates for becoming Elite. So instead of looking at the entire population, Yelp will only have to look at a select few.

Our Solution

Overall Idea

False Positives

Our idea to solve the problem is a simple idea. We will use the Yelp data to train a classification model. We then will use that classification model to predict which Yelpers should be classified as Elite. We then will submit those who are not Elite but were predicted to be Elite. The idea is that those Yelpers will most likely be the one that will make the jump to Elite.
